Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must fight against endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups that will help you survive longer.

The game features a unique evolution system where your character grows stronger with each enemy defeated. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose from various upgrades that completely change your playstyle and strategy.

With its simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors offers hours of addictive gameplay for both casual and hardcore gamers alike.

Mobile Controls

The game supports touch controls on mobile devices:

  • • Left side: Virtual joystick for movement
  • • Right side: Tap to use abilities
  • • Double tap: Dash in the direction you're moving
  • • Menu button: Access pause menu and settings

Pro Tips

Movement is Key

Never stop moving. Staying in one place will quickly get you surrounded by enemies. Practice circle strafing and using terrain to your advantage.

Prioritize Upgrades

Focus on a few key abilities rather than spreading points across many. A powerful build with synergistic abilities will outperform a jack-of-all-trades approach.

Collect Experience Orbs

Don't let experience orbs go to waste. Make sure to collect them after defeating enemies to level up faster and gain more abilities.

Use Dash Wisely

Your dash ability has a cooldown, so save it for critical moments when you need to escape or avoid damage from powerful attacks.

Area Damage is Powerful

Abilities that deal area-of-effect damage become increasingly valuable as the game progresses and more enemies appear on screen.
